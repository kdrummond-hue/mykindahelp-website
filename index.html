About Us

Who We Are

MyKindAHelp is a modern business support and optimization firm based in Tampa, Florida. We specialize in helping small businesses grow, operate efficiently, and adopt artificial intelligence without confusion or risk.

Founded on the belief that "good help is hard to find," we set out to change that. Our team brings hands-on experience across multiple industries, combining traditional business expertise with cutting-edge technology solutions.

We provide on-demand support for professionals and small businesses who need flexible, cost-effective help—without the overhead of full-time staff.
